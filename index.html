Exec Summary

Grow ARR by improving penetration, increasing coverage, and focusing the field where it matters most.

EMEA has a large addressable market, low customer penetration, and an AD footprint that is too light for the size of the opportunity. The FY26 move is to scale coverage, run the field through practical sub-regions, and focus leadership attention on Tier 1 countries, priority verticals, and the Top 100 portfolio.

01

ARR is growing, but penetration is still light.

We have meaningful momentum in Enterprise, Mid-Market, and Digital Natives, but the current customer base still covers only a small share of total EMEA accounts.

02

The opportunity is in whitespace.

The gap between TAM and current customers is what creates the growth case. We should treat whitespace as a coverage and focus problem, not just a pipeline problem.

03

Coverage needs to move from 53 ADs to 110 ADs.

That step-change lowers account load, improves customer coverage, and gives the field a more realistic path to expanding ARR across the highest-value countries and segments.

04

Focus follows a regional-first and country-tiered model.

Run the field through sub-regions, anchor execution in Germany, the UK, and France, concentrate on priority verticals, and use the Top 100 book as the sharp edge of execution.
